An introduction to the dawlf

The dolfare, or dawlf in English, is an furry species of canines that exist in the fantasy world of Laraybis.

These creatures are short, with pointy ears, long fluffy tails and a rounded long snoot with the two upper fangs poking out at all times.
Stuck to one of their limbs is a bone-like gem which provides them with magical affinities to the elements.

There are several different breeds of dawlf:

  • Regular, the baseline
  • Giant, a taller version
  • Royal, a stronger version
  • Sand, who adapted to heat
  • Aquatic, who adapted to water

There are two additional breeds which are further from the rest:

  • Feral, who are the descendants of the quadruped descendants that didn't turn anthro
  • Bastard, who are a cross breed between regular or giant and royal dawlfs
